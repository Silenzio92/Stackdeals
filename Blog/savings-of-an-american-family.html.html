That's not some fringe group of extreme couponers — that's a mainstream average, pulled from millions of ordinary shoppers buying groceries, clothing, and electronics.

At $1,465, you're looking at roughly one full paycheck for a median-income earner. A flight to Europe. Four months of car insurance. Money that already belongs to you — sitting unclaimed at checkout.
