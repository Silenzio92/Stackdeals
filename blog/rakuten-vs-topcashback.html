Rakuten vs TopCashback — Which Cashback Portal Is Better?

Both Rakuten and TopCashback are popular cashback platforms that reward you for shopping online. Instead of giving direct discounts at checkout, they return a percentage of your purchase after the transaction has been tracked and confirmed. The question most shoppers ask is simple: which portal actually saves you more money?

The Quick Answer

Use both. Neither Rakuten nor TopCashback consistently wins across all stores. Cashback rates change frequently, and the better option can vary from one retailer to another.

  • Sometimes Rakuten offers the better cashback rate
  • Sometimes TopCashback offers more
  • Promotions and retailer partnerships change regularly

If you only want to start with one platform, Rakuten is often considered easier for beginners because of its simple interface and popular browser extension.

How Cashback Portals Work

Both platforms follow a similar process:

1
Visit the cashback portal and search for the retailer
2
Click through to the retailer's website via the portal link
3
Shop normally — the purchase gets tracked automatically
4
Cashback enters a pending stage, then becomes payable
💡 Note

Cashback is usually not immediate. Purchases typically enter a pending stage before they become payable — this can take days or weeks depending on the retailer.

Important: Cashback Rates Are Not Fixed

A common mistake is assuming stores always have the same cashback percentages. In reality, rates can change because of:

  • retailer promotions and seasonal events
  • category-specific offers
  • limited-time campaigns
  • changes in affiliate partnerships

A store that offers a high rate today may offer a much lower rate next week. Fixed percentage comparison tables become outdated quickly.

Promo Codes and Cashback Rules

Promo code compatibility depends largely on retailer rules.

  • some retailers allow external promo codes
  • some only allow portal-listed codes
  • some unsupported codes may cancel cashback eligibility
⚠ Safer Approach

Use promo codes that are listed or approved by the cashback portal whenever possible to avoid losing your cashback.

Cashback Tracking Considerations

Cashback tracking can occasionally fail. Common reasons include:

  • ad blockers active in your browser
  • switching devices during checkout
  • cookie restrictions
  • unsupported promo codes
  • moving between app and browser environments

Following the portal's instructions carefully usually improves tracking reliability.

The Smartest Strategy

Instead of relying on a single cashback platform, compare before every purchase:

1
Compare current cashback rates on both portals before buying
2
Select the highest available offer
3
Follow the portal's tracking instructions carefully
4
Complete the purchase normally

This process usually takes less than a minute and can increase total savings meaningfully over time.

Final Verdict

There is no universally "best" cashback portal. The biggest savings do not come from loyalty to one platform — they come from consistently checking your options.

  • Choose Rakuten if you want simplicity, easy setup, and beginner-friendly tools
  • Choose TopCashback if you want flexibility, more control, and a comparison-focused approach
  • Use both for the strongest long-term strategy
